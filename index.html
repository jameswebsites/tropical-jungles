About Tropical Jungles

Where are they found?

Tropical Jungles are typically found in Brazil, The Democratic Republic of Congo, Indonesia, Hawaii and islands of the pacific and caribbean

What animals are found?

Jaguars, sloths, poison dart frogs, capybaras, gorillas, macaw, green anacondas, harpy eagles, howler monkeys, okapis, glass frogs, toucans, morphos, tapirs, amazon river dolphins, chimpanzees, emerald tree boas, lemurs, orangutans, red-eyed tree frogs, spider monkey, squirrel monkeys, tigers and anteaters.
